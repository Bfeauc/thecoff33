Earl Grey Tea

Earl Grey tea is known for its unique and aromatic profile. It begins with a base of black tea, which contributes a robust and slightly astringent flavor. What sets Earl Grey apart is the addition of bergamot oil, derived from the rind of the bergamot orange. This infusion imparts a fragrant, citrusy, and floral aroma to the tea.
