ABOUT OUR COMPANY

THE COFFEE

Grind The Essentials In a fast-paced world it is easy to lose sight of what truly matters.


THE COFF33 provides a place of solace where people can simply slow down and enjoy a high-quality cup of coffee. This philosophy is reflected within our tagline. By utilizing the word ‘Grind’ which has a double meaning: ‘Grind’ as in the day-to-day hustle that people go through, and ‘Grind’ as a key step in the coffee making process, THE COFF33 inspires people to embrace life’s essentials in the midst of their busy lifestyles through each cup of coffee we serve.
